About English in Doses

Master English in bite-sized sessions — because small, regular doses are more powerful than occasional marathons.

Our Approach to Language Learning

Why "English in Doses"?

The name "English in Doses" reflects our core teaching philosophy. We firmly believe that multiple, regular, short practice sessions with low stress levels are more effective than single, long, tiring sessions each week.

This approach is based on both extensive teaching experience and fundamental principles of language acquisition. Regular engagement with the language in a relaxed, supportive environment leads to better retention and more natural language use.

Just like medicine works better when taken in the right doses at regular intervals, language learning is most effective when done consistently in manageable amounts.

🎯

Tailored Learning

We understand that each student has unique goals, interests, and learning styles. Our approach prioritises understanding your specific needs and tailoring lessons accordingly, rather than following a one-size-fits-all curriculum.

⏱️

Time-Efficient

Our short, focused lessons are designed to fit into your busy schedule. You don't need to set aside large blocks of time to make progress—just consistent, manageable practice sessions that work for you.

💬

Real-World Focus

We focus on practical, useful English that you can immediately apply in real-life situations. We use authentic materials and relevant scenarios rather than unrealistic textbook examples.

Who We Serve

English in Doses is designed specifically for:

  • Adult Learners Students 17 years and older who are motivated to improve their English.
  • Intermediate+ Level Students at B1 level and above who already have a foundation in English.
  • Busy Professionals People with limited time who benefit from flexible, efficient learning.
  • Practical Learners Those who need English for work, travel, or living in English-speaking countries.

What We Offer

Conversation Classes

Our primary focus is on helping students improve their speaking skills through regular conversation practice. These sessions can cover a wide range of topics based on your interests and needs:

  • Business English Meetings, presentations, negotiations, emails, and workplace communication.
  • Travel English Navigating airports, hotels, restaurants, and tourist attractions.
  • Daily English Everyday conversations, social interactions, and practical situations.
  • Custom Topics Lessons tailored to your specific interests, industry, or learning goals.

Free Learning Resources

In addition to our conversation classes, we provide a variety of free resources to support your learning journey:

  • Grammar explanations designed for practical use.
  • Interactive practice activities with immediate feedback.
  • Conversation starters and useful phrases.
  • Progress tracking to monitor your improvement.

Coming soon: We plan to offer exam preparation resources in the future, helping students prepare for internationally recognised English proficiency exams.

Katerina Samara

Hello! I'm Katerina Samara, the founder of English in Doses. I've been teaching English since 2013 and moved to online teaching in 2020, which has allowed me to connect with students from all over the world.

I'm half Greek, half English, with experiences living and studying in both countries. Currently, I'm based in Brazil, where I continue to teach English online to adult students. My multicultural background gives me unique insights into the challenges of language learning.

Throughout my career, I've taught students of all ages and levels, but now I specialise in working with adult learners at intermediate (B1) level and above. I focus primarily on conversation classes covering a wide range of topics including travel, business, and daily English usage.

My teaching approach is student-centered, focusing on creating a relaxed, safe environment where you can speak freely and gain confidence. I believe in using English rather than just talking about it, and I make sure my examples and activities are relevant to real-life situations.

When I'm not teaching, I enjoy exploring nature, travelling, learning languages, and relaxing with a good cup of coffee.
