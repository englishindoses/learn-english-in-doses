About This Course

This course will prepare you to travel confidently using English. You will learn practical phrases, essential vocabulary, and key grammar for airports, hotels, restaurants, transportation and more.

10 Units ‚Ä¢ 41 Lessons ‚Ä¢ Beginner Level ‚Ä¢ 60-minute lessons

What You Will Learn

  • Understand and respond to common questions
  • Use practical phrases you'll actually need
  • Handle real travel situations confidently

Course Units

Work through the units in order, or jump to the topics you need most for your next trip.

Unit 1: Airport and Airplane Communication

Navigate airports confidently, from check-in through immigration.

  • Lesson 1: Check-in, Security, and Boarding
  • Lesson 2: On the Airplane
  • Lesson 3: Immigration and Customs
  • Lesson 4: Baggage Claim and Problems

Unit 2: At the Hotel - Check-in to Check-out

Master all essential hotel interactions from reservations to checking out.

  • Lesson 1: Making Hotel Reservations
  • Lesson 2: Hotel Check-in
  • Lesson 3: Hotel Services and Amenities
  • Lesson 4: Hotel Check-out

Unit 3: Hotel Issues and Requests

Handle common hotel problems and make special requests confidently.

  • Lesson 1: Requesting Extra Items and Amenities
  • Lesson 2: Reporting Room Problems
  • Lesson 3: WiFi and Technology Issues
  • Lesson 4: Meals, Noise, and Other Concerns

Unit 4: Making Reservations and Booking Services

Book various services and make appointments with confidence.

  • Lesson 1: Restaurant Reservations
  • Lesson 2: Booking Tours and Activities
  • Lesson 3: Appointments and Services
  • Lesson 4: Confirming and Modifying Bookings

Unit 5: Getting Directions and Using Transportation

Navigate unfamiliar places and use various transportation options.

  • Lesson 1: Asking for Locations and Basic Directions
  • Lesson 2: Using Public Transportation
  • Lesson 3: Taxis and Ride Services
  • Lesson 4: Giving Your Location and Getting Help

Unit 6: Ordering in Restaurants and Cafes

Master dining out in English-speaking environments.

  • Lesson 1: Reading Menus and Asking About Food
  • Lesson 2: Placing Orders and Making Requests
  • Lesson 3: During the Meal - Requests and Problems
  • Lesson 4: Paying the Bill and Restaurant Etiquette

Unit 7: Getting Help When You Have Problems

Essential language for handling emergencies and difficult situations.

  • Lesson 1: When You're Lost
  • Lesson 2: Lost and Stolen Items
  • Lesson 3: Medical Emergencies and Getting a Doctor
  • Lesson 4: Contacting Police and Authorities

Unit 8: Tours and Excursions

Book, participate in, and discuss guided tours and excursions.

  • Lesson 1: Finding and Booking Tours
  • Lesson 2: Tour Details and Logistics
  • Lesson 3: During the Tour - Asking Questions
  • Lesson 4: Tour Feedback and Problems

Unit 9: Describing Places and Giving Opinions

Describe locations and share your thoughts about places you visit.

  • Lesson 1: Describing Places with Simple Adjectives
  • Lesson 2: Recommending Places
  • Lesson 3: Describing Your City and Country
  • Lesson 4: Sharing Likes and Dislikes About Places

Unit 10: Travel Experiences and Sharing Trip Stories

Talk about past travels and future trip plans.

  • Lesson 1: Talking About Where You Went
  • Lesson 2: Describing What You Saw and Did
  • Lesson 3: Saying What You Liked
  • Lesson 4: Talking About Future Travel Plans
  • Lesson 5: Review and Travel Conversation Practice
