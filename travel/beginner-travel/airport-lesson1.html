Good to know: At security, you don't need to say much. Just follow instructions and say "Thank you" when you collect your things. If you don't understand, say: "Sorry, can you repeat that?" or "More slowly, please."

Lesson Summary

Key Phrases from This Lesson

Structure Use Example
I'd like to... Polite requests I'd like to check in, please.
Can I have...? Asking for things Can I have an aisle seat?
Where is...? Finding places Where is Gate 15?

Remember! Always be polite at the airport. Say "please" and "thank you". If you don't understand something, it's OK to ask: "Sorry, can you repeat that?"
