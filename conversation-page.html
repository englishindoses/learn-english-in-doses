English Conversation Practice

Improve Your Speaking Confidence

Regular conversation practice is one of the most effective ways to improve your English fluency. This section offers both self-study conversation resources and opportunities to practice speaking with a qualified teacher in a supportive environment.

Whether you need to improve your English for work, travel, or everyday life, personalized conversation practice will help you gain confidence and speak more naturally.

Book a Conversation Session

Why Practice Conversation?

  • Build confidence speaking in real-life situations
  • Learn natural expressions that native speakers actually use
  • Improve your pronunciation and reduce your accent
  • Practice speaking fluently without translating in your head
  • Get personalized feedback on your specific language challenges

Frequently Asked Questions

What platform do we use for sessions? expand_more
Sessions take place via Zoom or Google Meet, depending on your preference. Both platforms are free to use and I'll send you a link before our scheduled session.
How long are the sessions? expand_more
Standard sessions are 45 minutes long, which provides enough time for meaningful conversation practice without causing fatigue.
Do I need to prepare anything? expand_more
No preparation is required, though I may suggest topics based on your interests and goals. Just come ready to speak and practice your English in a relaxed environment.
Will you correct all my mistakes? expand_more
I focus on errors that impact communication rather than correcting every small mistake, which would interrupt the natural flow of conversation. After our session, I'll provide a summary of recurring errors and suggestions for improvement.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule? expand_more
Please provide at least 24 hours' notice to reschedule without charge. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee.
Can we focus on specific situations? expand_more
Absolutely! Whether you need to practice for job interviews, presentations, or social situations, we can tailor our sessions to your needs. Just mention your specific goals when booking.
