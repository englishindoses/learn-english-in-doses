# English in Doses — Instagram Post & Carousel Design Guidelines

> This document is the single source of truth for anyone (or any AI) creating Instagram content for the **English in Doses** brand. Follow it to ensure every post and carousel looks like it belongs to the same family.

---

## 1. Brand Overview

| | |
|---|---|
| **Brand name** | English in Doses |
| **Tagline** | Learn English in Short, Effective Sessions |
| **Audience** | Adult ESL learners (beginner → advanced) |
| **Tone** | Friendly, encouraging, clear, expert but never intimidating |
| **Core idea** | Small, regular practice beats infrequent marathon sessions |

---

## 2. Logos & Brand Assets

Always use the official logo. Never recreate it from scratch.

| Asset | File | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| Primary logo (circular) | `my-logo.svg` | All posts where the background is dark or coloured |
| Transparent logo | `my-logo-transp.svg` | White or light backgrounds |

**Logo placement rules:**
- Always place the logo in the **top-left or bottom-right** corner of every slide / post.
- Minimum size: **60 × 60 px** at 1080 px canvas width (never smaller — it must be readable on a phone screen).
- Maintain a clear margin of at least **24 px** between the logo and any edge.
- Never stretch, rotate, recolour, or add effects to the logo.

---

## 3. Canvas Sizes

| Format | Dimensions | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| Square post | 1080 × 1080 px | Single tips, quotes, vocabulary words |
| Portrait post / Reel cover | 1080 × 1350 px | Standalone lessons, announcements |
| Carousel slide | 1080 × 1080 px | Multi-slide lessons, grammar breakdowns |
| Story | 1080 × 1920 px | Polls, quizzes, behind-the-scenes |

Resolution: **72 dpi** (screen), exported as **PNG** (for graphics) or **JPG** (for photo-heavy).

## 4. Colour Palette

### 4.1 Primary Palette — Use on every post

| Name | Hex | RGB | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Navy (Brand Dark) | `#1B263B` | 27, 38, 59 | Backgrounds, headers, text on light slides |
| Sky Blue (Accent) | `#3498db` | 52, 152, 219 | Highlights, icons, dividers, CTA buttons |
| Warm Orange (Secondary) | `#f07d3e` | 240, 125, 62 | CTAs, emphasis, accent pops |
| Off-White | `#f8f9fa` | 248, 249, 250 | Card fills, light slide backgrounds |
| Pure White | `#ffffff` | 255, 255, 255 | Text on dark backgrounds |
| Dark Body Text | `#333333` | 51, 51, 51 | Body copy on light slides |

### 4.2 Level Colours — Use when content belongs to a specific level

| Level | Primary | Secondary | Card Fill | When to use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Beginner | `#4CAF50` (green) | `#f39c12` (orange) | `#f0fff5` (mint) | Posts tagged "beginner" or "A1/A2" |
| Intermediate | `#fca421` (amber) | `#3498db` (blue) | `#fff8e1` (cream) | Posts tagged "intermediate" or "B1/B2" |
| Advanced | `#1B263B` (navy) | `#3498db` (blue) | `#eef2f7` (pale blue-grey) | Posts tagged "advanced" or "C1/C2" |
| Conversation / Travel | `#26A69A` (teal) | `#f5ae51` (soft orange) | `#f0f9f6` (pale teal) | Conversation tips, travel English |

### 4.3 Functional Colours

| Purpose | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Correct / Success | `#28a745` | Quiz answers, "Well done" feedback |
| Error / Incorrect | `#dc3545` | Common mistakes callouts |
| Note / Tip background | `#e8f4fd` | Info boxes, grammar tips |
| Note border | `#bde0fe` | Left accent border on tip boxes |
| WhatsApp CTA | `#25D366` | WhatsApp contact slide (if used) |

### 4.4 Colour Pairing Rules

- **Dark background (Navy `#1B263B`) + White text** — primary pairing for eye-catching slides.
- **Off-White `#f8f9fa` + Navy `#1B263B` text** — clean, readable content slides.
- **Sky Blue `#3498db` + White text** — accent/highlight slides, section dividers.
- **Warm Orange `#f07d3e` + White text** — CTA slides ("Follow for more", "Book a lesson").
- Never place Orange on Blue or Blue on Orange as background + body text — contrast is insufficient for body copy.
- Always check that body text passes **WCAG AA contrast** (4.5:1 minimum).

## 5. Typography

### 5.1 Font Families

| Font | Weight(s) | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Montserrat** | 700 (Bold), 600 (SemiBold), 400 (Regular) | All headings, slide titles, labels |
| **Open Sans** | 600 (SemiBold), 400 (Regular) | Body copy, explanations, captions |
| **Source Code Pro** | 400 | English examples shown in a "code block" style (optional, for grammar patterns) |

Both Montserrat and Open Sans are free on Google Fonts and available in Canva.

### 5.2 Type Scale (at 1080 × 1080 px canvas)

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Slide number / label | Montserrat | 18 px | 600 | Accent colour |
| Subtitle / category tag | Montserrat | 22–26 px | 600 | Accent or white |
| Main slide title | Montserrat | 48–64 px | 700 | White or Navy |
| Section heading | Montserrat | 34–40 px | 700 | Navy or White |
| Body copy | Open Sans | 26–30 px | 400 | `#333333` or White |
| Callout / emphasis | Open Sans | 28–32 px | 600 | Accent colour |
| Grammar example | Source Code Pro or Open Sans italic | 24–28 px | 400 | Navy or Dark |
| Caption / fine print | Open Sans | 18–20 px | 400 | `#6c757d` or white 70% opacity |

> **Mobile rule:** Nothing smaller than **18 px** on a 1080 px canvas — it will be unreadable on a phone at half-size.

### 5.3 Text Styling Rules

- **Left-align** body text. Centre only slide titles and single-word/short headlines.
- Line height: **1.4–1.6×** the font size.
- Letter spacing on all-caps labels: **+0.08 em**.
- Never use more than **2 font families** on a single slide.
- Max **3 font sizes** per slide (title + body + label/caption).

## 6. Layout & Grid

### 6.1 Safe Zone

Keep all text and key visuals inside a **980 × 980 px** safe zone (50 px margin on all sides of a 1080 × 1080 canvas). Instagram crops previews slightly — the safe zone protects your content.

### 6.2 Standard Slide Layout

```
┌─────────────────────────────────┐
│  [LOGO]          [Slide X of Y] │  ← Header strip (80–100 px tall)
├─────────────────────────────────┤
│                                 │
│       MAIN CONTENT AREA         │
│   (title + body + visuals)      │
│                                 │
├─────────────────────────────────┤
│  englishindoses.com  [tagline]  │  ← Footer strip (60–80 px tall)
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

- **Header strip:** Brand Navy `#1B263B`, holds the logo (left) and slide counter (right) in white.
- **Main area:** Background varies by content type (see §7). Padded 40 px on all sides.
- **Footer strip:** Slightly lighter navy or the level colour. Holds website URL and optional short tagline in white, 70% opacity.

### 6.3 Content Zones

Split the main area into a simple column or two-column grid:

**Single-column (most slides):**
- Title at top, body below, optional icon or illustration centred.

**Two-column (comparison / before–after / correct–incorrect):**
- Left column: 50% width, right column: 50% width, 24 px gutter.
- Use a vertical divider line (`#ddd` or the level accent colour, 2 px).

## 7. Slide & Card Styles

### 7.1 Cover Slide (Slide 1 of any carousel)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Background | Gradient: Navy `#1B263B` → Sky Blue `#3498db` (top-left to bottom-right, 135°) |
| Title | Montserrat 700, 56–64 px, White |
| Subtitle / topic tag | Montserrat 600, 24 px, Warm Orange `#f07d3e` |
| Logo | Top-left, white/transparent version |
| Slide counter | Hidden (no counter on the cover) |
| Optional illustration | Right side, `woman-with-speech-transp.svg` or topic-relevant icon |

### 7.2 Content Slide (Slides 2–N)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Background | Off-White `#f8f9fa` |
| Header strip | Navy `#1B263B` |
| Title | Montserrat 700, 40–48 px, Navy |
| Body | Open Sans 400, 26–28 px, `#333333` |
| Left accent border on boxes | 5 px solid — accent colour (Sky Blue or level colour) |
| Card / info box | Background White `#ffffff`, border-radius 12 px, shadow `0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` |
| Divider line | 2 px, `#ddd` or level accent |

### 7.3 Example / Grammar Box

Use this whenever showing a grammar pattern or English example:

```
┌────────────────────────────────┐
│ ▌ EXAMPLE                      │  ← Accent-coloured left border (5 px)
│                                │     Label: Montserrat 600, 18 px, Accent
│  "She has been studying         │     Body: Open Sans italic, 26 px, Navy
│   English for two years."      │
└────────────────────────────────┘
```

- Background: Note blue `#e8f4fd` (or level card fill colour).
- Border-radius: 8 px.
- Padding: 20 px 24 px.

### 7.4 Correct vs. Incorrect Box

| | Correct | Incorrect |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `rgba(76,175,80,0.12)` | `rgba(220,53,69,0.12)` |
| Border-left | `#28a745` 5 px | `#dc3545` 5 px |
| Icon | ✓ in `#28a745` | ✗ in `#dc3545` |
| Label | "CORRECT" — Montserrat 600, `#28a745` | "INCORRECT" — Montserrat 600, `#dc3545` |
| Text | Open Sans 400, `#333333` | Open Sans 400, `#333333` |

### 7.5 Tip / Note Box

Use for grammar tips, memory tricks, or study advice:

- Background: `#e8f4fd`
- Border-left: `#3498db` 5 px
- Icon: 💡 or an info circle icon in `#3498db`
- Label: "TIP" or "NOTE" — Montserrat 700, 16 px, `#3498db`, letter-spaced
- Body: Open Sans 400, 24–26 px, `#333333`

### 7.6 Call-to-Action Slide (Last slide of every carousel)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Background | Warm Orange `#f07d3e` (solid) |
| Headline | Montserrat 700, 52 px, White |
| Sub-text | Open Sans 400, 26 px, White 90% opacity |
| Button style | White pill button (border-radius 50 px), Navy `#1B263B` text, Montserrat 600 |
| Logo | Bottom-centre or bottom-right, white transparent version |

Typical CTA copy options:
- "Follow for daily English tips"
- "Save this post to study later"
- "Book a free trial lesson — link in bio"
- "Share this with someone learning English"

## 8. Icons & Illustrations

- Use **flat, line-style icons** in the brand accent colour (`#3498db` or the level colour). Avoid 3D, skeuomorphic, or highly detailed icons.
- Icon size on a content slide: **40–60 px**. On a full-icon cover: up to **200 px**.
- The brand illustration (`woman-with-speech-transp.svg`) can appear on cover slides or CTA slides — right-aligned, overlapping the bottom edge slightly for depth.
- For level-specific posts, pair the level colour with a simple relevant icon (e.g., a seedling for Beginner, a rising chart for Intermediate, a mortarboard for Advanced, a speech bubble for Conversation).

## 9. Carousel Structure Templates

### Template A — Grammar Rule Carousel (5–7 slides)

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 — Cover | Topic title + subtitle (e.g., "Present Perfect vs. Past Simple") |
| 2 — The Rule | One clear, short rule statement + example box |
| 3 — When to Use It | Bullet list (max 3 bullets) with icon per bullet |
| 4 — Common Mistake | Correct vs. Incorrect boxes side-by-side or stacked |
| 5 — Practice Example | Fill-in or rewrite exercise (answer revealed on next slide or in caption) |
| 6 — Quick Summary | Key points as 3–4 numbered cards |
| 7 — CTA | "Save & Follow" CTA slide |

### Template B — Vocabulary / Word of the Day (3–5 slides)

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 — Cover | Word in large Montserrat 700 + phonetic spelling smaller, level badge |
| 2 — Definition | Clear definition + part of speech badge (noun, verb, etc.) |
| 3 — Examples | 2–3 example sentences in the grammar-example box style |
| 4 — Synonyms / Antonyms | Two-column layout |
| 5 — CTA | "Learn a new word every week — follow us" |

### Template C — Quick Tip (Single Post or 2 slides)

- Single image, dark Navy background or cover gradient.
- Large, punchy headline (Montserrat 700, 52–64 px).
- One-line explanation below (Open Sans 400, 26 px, white 80%).
- Logo bottom-right, website URL bottom-left.

### Template D — Study Tip / Advice Post (4–6 slides)

| Slide | Content |
|---|---|
| 1 — Cover | Bold question or statement (e.g., "Why you're not improving your English") |
| 2–N — Point slides | One point per slide: number badge + heading + 2-line explanation |
| Last — CTA | Warm Orange CTA slide |

## 10. Slide Counter Style

Displayed in the header strip, top-right corner.

- Format: **"2 / 6"** or **"Slide 2 of 6"**
- Font: Montserrat 600, 18 px, White 70% opacity
- No counter on the Cover slide (slide 1).

---

## 11. Level Badge / Tag

When a post belongs to a specific level, include a small pill badge on the cover slide (top-right area, inside the safe zone, below the slide counter):

- Shape: Pill (border-radius 50 px), height 32 px, horizontal padding 14 px
- Background: Level primary colour (see §4.2)
- Text: "BEGINNER", "INTERMEDIATE", "ADVANCED", or "CONVERSATION" — Montserrat 700, 14 px, White, letter-spaced +0.05 em

## 12. Shadows & Depth

| Element | Shadow |
|---|---|
| Content cards on light backgrounds | `0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` |
| Cover slide illustration | `0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.20)` |
| Buttons (when shown) | `0 4px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.15)` |
| Avoid on: | Dark / coloured full-bleed slides (shadow is invisible and adds clutter) |

## 13. Spacing & Padding

| Context | Value |
|---|---|
| Edge-to-content margin | 50 px (safe zone boundary) |
| Padding inside cards/boxes | 20–30 px |
| Gap between elements | 24 px |
| Gap between card rows | 32 px |
| Section title margin-bottom | 16 px |
| Header strip height | 80–100 px |
| Footer strip height | 60–80 px |

## 14. Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- Keep each slide to **one main idea**.
- Use **bullet points sparingly** — max 3–4 per slide, short phrases only.
- Always include the **logo** on every slide.
- Always include a **CTA slide** at the end of every carousel.
- Use the **level colour system** consistently within a single post series.
- Add a **"Save this post"** call-to-action in the caption for carousels.
- Test your design by **shrinking it to phone size** — all text must still be readable.

### Don't:
- Don't use more than **3 colours** on a single slide (excluding white and black).
- Don't put more than **~40 words** of body copy on a single slide.
- Don't use fonts other than Montserrat, Open Sans, and Source Code Pro.
- Don't use gradients other than the approved Navy → Sky Blue brand gradient.
- Don't use clip art, watermarked stock imagery, or highly decorative borders.
- Don't place text over a busy background without a semi-transparent overlay.
- Don't change the logo's proportions or colours.

## 15. Caption & Hashtag Guidelines

(For reference when creating the accompanying post caption)

**Caption structure:**
1. Hook — first line (visible before "more") should be a question or bold statement.
2. Value — 3–5 lines explaining the post's key takeaway.
3. CTA — "Save this post", "Follow for more", or "Book a free trial — link in bio".
4. Hashtags — 5–10 targeted, on a separate line or at the end.

**Suggested hashtag groups:**

*Grammar:* `#englishgrammar #learnenglish #esl #eslgrammar #englishtips #grammarlesson #englishlearning`

*Vocabulary:* `#englishvocabulary #wordoftheday #learnenglishonline #vocabularybuilding #englishwords`

*General / Brand:* `#englishindoses #learnenglishwithtips #adultesl #onlineenglish #englishtutor`
