Speaking Tips

  • Answer fully: Try to answer with 2-3 sentences, not just "yes" or "no"
  • Be confident: Don't worry about small mistakes - focus on communicating
  • Be creative: If you don't know a word, try to explain it another way

Example Answer

Question: What is your favourite food?

"My favourite food is pasta. I really love spaghetti with tomato sauce and cheese. I usually eat it once a week, usually on weekends. It's easy to cook and always delicious!"
