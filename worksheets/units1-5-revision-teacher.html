📋 Units 1–5 Revision – Teacher's Guide

How to use this guide

This page accompanies the student worksheet. It contains the oral questions for Activity 6 (Listening) and guidance for Activity 7 (Speaking).

Do not let the student see this page during the lesson.

👂 Activity 6: Oral Questions

Read these questions aloud. Do not let the student see the text.

# Read this question aloud Target answer
1 "What's this in English?" A – It's an umbrella.
2 "Are you from Japan?" B – Yes, I'm from Tokyo.
3 "What's her name?" C – Daniela.
4 "Is she from England?" B – No, she isn't.
5 "What's his job?" A – He's a nurse.
6 "What's your job?" B – I'm a nurse.
7 "What's her favourite food?" A – She likes pizza.
8 "Do you speak Spanish?" A – No, I don't.

🎤 Activity 7: Production (Speaking)

Ask these questions naturally. If the student is slow to process, offer the starting sound of the answer.

# Question Notes
1 What's your name? Prompt: "My name's…" or "I'm…"
2 Where are you from? Prompt: "I'm from…"
3 Are you a student? Prompt: "Yes, I am." / "No, I'm not. I'm a…"
4 Do you like football? Prompt: "Yes, I do." / "No, I don't."
5 What's your phone number? Prompt: "It's…" or "My number is…"
6 What's your favourite food and drink? Prompt: "I like…" / "My favourite food is…"
7 Are you married or single? Prompt: "I'm married." / "I'm single."
8 Do you speak Spanish or English? Prompt: "I speak…" / "Yes, I do." / "No, I don't."
