Quick Tip: Present Perfect Progressive

Remember to use Present Perfect Progressive when talking about an ongoing action that started in the past and continues into the present:

"I have been studying English for three years."

vs. Present Perfect Simple for completed actions or ongoing states:

"I have studied three different languages."
