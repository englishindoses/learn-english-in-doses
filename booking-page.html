Frequently Asked Questions

What questions will you ask me?

I will ask about your current English level, your goals (for example, work, travel, exams, or general improvement), areas you want to focus on (speaking, writing, grammar, vocabulary), and your availability. This helps me prepare lessons that are right for you from day one.

What is your availability?

I teach online from Monday to Friday, with some flexibility on weekends. When you message me, tell me your time zone and preferred days and times, and I will let you know what slots I have available.

How much does a lesson cost?

Pricing depends on the type of course and how many lessons you book. I offer flexible packages and discounts for students who book multiple lessons in advance. Send me a message and I will share the current prices with you.

Is there a cancellation policy?

Yes. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please let me know at least 24 hours in advance and we can move the lesson to another time with no charge. Cancellations with less notice may still be charged, as the time has been reserved for you.

Do I need any materials or software?

You only need a device with a camera and microphone, a stable internet connection, and a free video call app (such as Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams). I will provide all the learning materials you need for our lessons.
