Multiple Choice Questions

Choose the correct option to complete each conditional sentence.

1. If you heat ice, it _____. (Zero conditional)

B. will melt
C. would melt

2. If it rains tomorrow, we _____ to the museum instead of the park. (First conditional)

D. would have gone

3. If I _____ rich, I would buy a private island. (Second conditional)

B. will be

D. had been

4. If she had studied harder, she _____ the exam. (Third conditional)

D. would have passed
