Understand menu items and ask about ingredients confidently

Warm-up Questions (3 minutes)

  1. What's your favourite Brazilian restaurant dish?
  2. Do you have any food allergies or dietary restrictions?
  3. Look at this menu title: "Today's Specials." What questions might you ask?

üìù Key Phrases to Learn Today

  • What's in this dish?
  • Do you have vegetarian options?
  • Is this spicy?
  • What do you recommend?
  • I'm allergic to...
  • Does this contain nuts/dairy/gluten?

Activity 3: Pronunciation Practice

Practice these sounds that are often difficult for Brazilian English learners:

üîä TH Sounds

Practice these words slowly:

  • "this" - Put your tongue between your teeth
  • "that" - Same tongue position
  • "with" - Don't say "wif" - keep the 'th' sound

üîä Final Consonants

Don't drop the final sounds:

  • "dish" - End with a clear 'sh' sound, not "dishe"
  • "meat" - End with a strong 't' sound
  • "recommend" - End with a clear 'd' sound
