I Like / I Don't Like

Practice Activities

Let's practise talking about things you like and don't like! Complete the activities below.

The Language

Read this conversation between two friends:

Ana: Hi Carlos! Do you want some coffee?

Carlos: No, thanks. I don't like coffee.

Ana: Really? I like coffee a lot! Do you like tea?

Carlos: Yes, I do! I like green tea.

Ana: Me too! Do you like cooking?

Carlos: No, I don't. I don't like cooking. But I like eating!
