Beginner ESL Grammar Course

Welcome to beginner English grammar. This course teaches you the basic grammar rules you need for everyday life. You will learn step by step with clear examples and practice.

This course will help you:

  • Use basic English grammar correctly in everyday situations.
  • Make simple sentences with correct word order and ask questions.
  • Understand simple English texts and conversations.

Essential Beginner Grammar (A1–A2)

  • Subject Pronouns and the Verb "Be" (I am, you are, he is)
  • Articles: A, An, The
  • Singular and Plural Nouns
  • Present Simple
  • Present Continuous (I am doing)
  • Possessives: My, Your, His, Her, Our, Their
  • Countable and Uncountable Nouns (some, any, much, many)
  • Past Simple (Regular and Irregular Verbs)
  • Prepositions of Time and Place (in, on, at)
  • There Is / There Are
