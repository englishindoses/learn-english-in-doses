Intermediate ESL Grammar Course

Welcome to the intermediate grammar course. This course is designed for learners who have mastered the basics and are ready to develop a deeper understanding of English grammar structures and usage. Each module builds on your existing knowledge, guiding you through more complex grammar patterns and helping you express yourself with greater precision and fluency.

This course will help you:

  • Express yourself with greater accuracy and confidence.
  • Master more complex tense combinations.
  • Create sophisticated sentence structures.
  • Understand the subtle differences between similar grammar patterns.
  • Build a strong foundation for advanced English study.

Course Structure

Each topic includes:

  1. Introduction - Overview of the grammatical concept.
  2. Explanation - Structure, use and exceptions.
  3. Examples - Authentic usage in various contexts.
  4. Native Speaker Note - Quick notes about how native speakers use the grammar in real life.
  5. Common Errors - Typical mistakes and how to avoid them.
  6. Practice Exercises - Test your knowledge!
  7. Useful tips and advice - Extra tips about how to learn and use the grammar.
  8. Extra Practice - After each topic you can access multiple, short extra practice activities.

Getting Started

Begin with module one to follow the full structure of the course, or simply skip ahead to the specific grammar rule you are looking for. Make sure you complete the practice activities. Regular practice is essential for mastering these grammar structures!

If you find a topic too challenging, consider reviewing the related beginner-level topics first. If a topic seems too easy, you may be ready to move to the advanced course.
