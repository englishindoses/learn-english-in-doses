Simple Past Tense Practice Activities

Practice Makes Perfect

Welcome to the practice section for the simple past tense! Complete these activities to solidify your understanding of when and how to use the simple past, distinguish it from other tenses, and avoid common errors.

Quick Review

The simple past tense is used to talk about:

  • Completed actions in the past
  • Series of completed actions in the past
  • Duration in the past that is finished
  • Habits or repeated actions in the past

For regular verbs, we add -ed to the base form. For irregular verbs, we use their specific past forms (went, saw, took, etc.).
